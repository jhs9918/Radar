REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
City of Riverton — Integrated Financial Management System Upgrade

RFP Number: RIV-2024-FMS-001
Issue Date: February 1, 2024
Proposal Due: March 15, 2024, 4:00 PM CST

1. INTRODUCTION
The City of Riverton seeks proposals to upgrade its Integrated Financial Management System.
The City currently operates SAP ECC 6.0 and wishes to modernize to a current-generation
ERP solution while preserving all existing configurations, custom reports, and workflows.

2. MANDATORY VENDOR QUALIFICATIONS
Bidders must meet ALL of the following criteria to be considered:

a) Product experience: Vendor must have successfully implemented SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud
   at a minimum of three (3) US municipal government clients within the past three (3) years.
   Note: the City currently uses SAP ECC 6.0; the upgrade must maintain all current
   SAP ECC modules and configurations with zero data loss.

b) Local presence: Vendor must maintain a physical staffed office within 50 miles of
   Riverton City Hall (123 Main Street, Riverton). Remote-only firms are not eligible.

c) References: All three client references must be US municipal governments with a
   population between 50,000 and 200,000 residents. References from counties, states,
   or federal agencies will not be accepted.

d) Certification: Designated project manager must hold a valid SAP Certified Application
   Associate — SAP S/4HANA for Financial Accounting certification at time of proposal.

3. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- SAP S/4HANA Municipal Experience: 40%
- Local Office Presence & On-site Availability: 20%
- Technical Implementation Approach: 25%
- Cost: 15%

4. SCOPE OF WORK
The selected vendor shall:
- Upgrade the existing SAP ECC 6.0 system to SAP S/4HANA, maintaining all current
  configuration, custom ABAP reports, and workflow integrations
- Ensure seamless transition from the current system with no disruption to City payroll,
  accounts payable, or general ledger processes during cutover
- Provide familiarity with the City's existing financial processes and chart of accounts
- Deliver training for 120 Finance Department staff

5. TIMELINE
- Proposals Due: March 15, 2024
- Vendor Presentations (shortlisted only): April 1–5, 2024
- Contract Award: May 1, 2024
- System Go-Live: December 31, 2024

6. BUDGET
The City has not disclosed a budget for this engagement. Vendors should propose a
total fixed-price cost inclusive of all licenses, implementation, and 3 years of support.
