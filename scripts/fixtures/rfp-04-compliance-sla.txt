REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
State Department of Health — Electronic Health Records Platform

RFP Number: DOH-2024-EHR-001
Issue Date: January 15, 2024
Proposal Due: March 1, 2024, 5:00 PM EST

1. INTRODUCTION
The State Department of Health seeks a qualified vendor to implement a cloud-based
Electronic Health Records (EHR) platform across 45 state-operated clinics serving
approximately 280,000 patients annually.

2. SCOPE OF WORK
The selected vendor shall:
- Implement a HIPAA-compliant cloud EHR platform
- Migrate historical patient records from the legacy on-premises system
- Integrate with the State's existing Medicaid billing system (MMIS)
- Provide staff training for 650+ clinical and administrative personnel
- Deliver all project documentation and source code in escrow

3. DATA RESIDENCY & COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
All patient data and backups MUST be stored exclusively within European Union data centers.
Data must not leave EU jurisdiction under any circumstances, including disaster recovery.
The vendor MUST achieve and maintain SOC 2 Type II certification within 90 days of
contract award. ISO 27001 certification is required at go-live.
HIPAA compliance with annual third-party audits at vendor's expense is mandatory.
Vendor shall undergo a penetration test conducted by a State-approved third party
no later than 30 days before go-live, and annually thereafter.

4. SERVICE LEVEL REQUIREMENTS
- System uptime: 99.95% measured monthly, excluding pre-approved maintenance windows
- Critical incident notification: issues affecting > 10 users must be reported within
  2 hours of detection, all others within 24 hours
- Page load response time: 95th percentile under 2 seconds under full production load
- Support: 24/7/365 helpdesk with dedicated account manager; average response < 4 hours
- Disaster recovery: RPO ≤ 1 hour, RTO ≤ 4 hours

5. CONTRACT TERMS
- Post-implementation support obligation: 5 years mandatory
- Performance penalties: $10,000 per hour of downtime exceeding the 99.95% SLA threshold
- Liquidated damages for late delivery: 0.5% of total contract value per calendar day
- Cost proposal must be submitted in a sealed separate envelope, distinct from the
  technical proposal; sealed envelopes will be opened only for shortlisted vendors

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA
- Technical Approach: 30%
- Compliance & Security Posture: 25%
- Relevant Healthcare Experience: 25%
- Cost: 20%

7. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
Proposals must include:
- Technical proposal (max 40 pages)
- Sealed cost envelope (separate from technical)
- SOC 2 Type II report or roadmap if not yet achieved
- HIPAA Security Risk Assessment conducted within 12 months
- Three healthcare client references (minimum 100,000 patients each)
- Proof of EU data center capacity

Submit via: State eProcurement portal by March 1, 2024 at 5:00 PM EST.
Late submissions will not be accepted.
